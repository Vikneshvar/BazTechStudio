Research Projects


  1. Public Health Risk Analysis and Geographical Mapping

    Tools Used: Arc GIS, SAS, Python, MS Excel

    Dataset: Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services, US Census Bureau

    This project establishes measures such as Emergency Vulnerability Index (EVI) and Healthcare Emergency Accessibility index (HEAI) to assess the risk associated with the frail elderly population living in a particular region based on their vulnerability and healthcare reachability.The socio-economic conditions, chronic illness, and old age make people highly vulnerable to natural or man-made disasters and hazards. In addition, the unavailability and inaccessibility of health care resources during emergency deprives the chances of saving them.
